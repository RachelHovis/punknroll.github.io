About Us

In New York City, there are many mobile businesses, a lot of which sell clothes. Ghost Fernandez, the soon to be founder of Punk 'N' Roll would regularly see these businesses while walking through the city but never found one that catered to her style. She loved all alternative styles, from goth to emo to grunge to scene to, of course, punk, so, she decided to start her own mobile business, called Punk 'N' Roll. Punk 'N' Roll is dedicated to providing affordable and good-quality alternative clothing for all people regardless of body type, size, or gender identity, because we believe that punk is for everyone. The truck, nicknamed "The Void", goes around the city selling alternative clothes and can also be rented out for events like conventions and markets. Our business has been beloved by many a punk rocker in the city for three years since the business first started.

Charity of the Month

Despite the rumors and misconceptions, the Punk community is very friendly and largely tries to put their best foot forward in helping their communities, and Punk 'N' Roll is no different. So, every month we donate a portion of all sales to a charity of our choice. With March being National Women's History month, we wanted this month's charity to go towards empowering women, so we've chosen NOW-NYC. This charity goes towards fighting for reproductive rights, promoting women in politics, and challenging the discrimination and violence that women face. If you want to donate directly, during your shopping experience with us you can simply round your change up or leave a tip if you so choose. You can find more information about NOW-NYC and the work that they do on their website here.

FAQs

What percent of money is donated to charity?
5% of all of our sales go the charity of the month every month, but additional donations are made by customers that may add more.
Do you do online orders?
Unfortunately, no we do not do online orders, if you would like to purchase something you must come to the truck and purchase it there.
Will you come to other cities or states?
As we are a small business, we only have one truck, so we can only stay in NYC for the time being, we do not have any plans to go to other states, but we may go to other cities for certain events that we are rented out to.
